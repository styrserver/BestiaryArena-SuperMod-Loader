About

Bestiary Arena SuperMod Loader is a powerful extension and enhancement of the original Bestiary Arena Mod Loader. This SuperMod builds on the foundation of the original loader, adding new features, improved mod support, and a modernized dashboard experience.

What is this?
This project is a community-driven SuperMod that extends the capabilities of the Bestiary Arena Mod Loader, making it easier to manage, customize, and enjoy mods for Bestiary Arena. It is designed for modders and players who want more control and a richer experience.
